about me


My name is Imogen Hunter, and I am a third year student studying a
Bachelor of Design (Honours) / Media (PR and Advertising) at the University
of New South Wales in Sydney, Australia. I’m at Concordia on Exchange for the
Fall Semester. Whilst I was born in regional New South Wales and now live in
Sydney, I spent much of my childhood growing up in the Pacific Islands, firstly
in Samoa and then in Fiji. These early experiences fostered a rich curiosity for
the visual arts. As an emerging designer and creative, I’m constantly pursuing
new, challenging experiences to extend my knowledge and creative skills.
